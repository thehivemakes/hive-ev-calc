Is a Home EV Charger Worth It?

A Level 2 home charger typically costs $500–1,500 for the unit plus $500–1,500 for installation. The savings come from charging at home electricity rates instead of public charging rates.

At $0.13/kWh home vs $0.35/kWh public, a driver using 300 kWh/month saves $66/month. The charger pays for itself in about 23 months, then saves $800+/year after that.
