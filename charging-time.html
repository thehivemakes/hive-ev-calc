How Long Does It Take to Charge an EV?

EV charging time depends on three factors: battery size, charger power, and charging efficiency. A 75 kWh battery charging from 20% to 80% needs 45 kWh of energy.

At Level 2 (7.7 kW), that takes about 6.5 hours. At a 150 kW DC fast charger, about 20 minutes. DC fast charging slows significantly above 80% state of charge to protect the battery.
