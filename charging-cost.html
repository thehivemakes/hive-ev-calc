How Much Does It Cost to Charge an EV?

The cost to charge an EV depends on your electricity rate and the vehicle's efficiency. At the US average of $0.13/kWh, a full charge of a 75 kWh battery costs about $9.75.

Most EVs get 3–4 miles per kWh, making the cost per mile about $0.03–0.04. Compare that to a gas car at $3.50/gallon and 30 mpg: $0.117 per mile. EVs cost about 70% less per mile to fuel.
