How Fast Do EV Batteries Degrade?

EV battery degradation averages 2–3% per year for most modern lithium-ion batteries. After 5 years, most EVs retain 85–90% of their original capacity.

Heat is the biggest enemy of battery longevity. Avoiding frequent DC fast charging and not keeping the battery at 100% for extended periods also helps preserve battery health.
